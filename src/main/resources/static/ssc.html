Syllabus Overview

We help you lay the strongest foundation for your upcoming SSC exam and ensure you achieve a sturdy preparation to fulfil your dreams of attaining a government job. We are the most preferred SSC coaching centre in Hyderabad where we help all aspirants crack the upcoming SSC with absolute ease. To apply for SSC, you can opt for an SSC online application by logging in to the official website. Once done, make sure you keep an eye out for any SSC notifications so you never miss anything. For exam dates, make sure you look for specific notifications, such as SSC CHSL notification or SSC CGL notification. Our teachers understand the SSC CHSL exam pattern very well and bring you chapter-wise tests and quizzes for thorough practice. We cover all the subjects that you will face in the SSC exam and even offer you the right strategy to perfectly attempt the exam for the best results.

SSC syllabus can be divided into Quantitative Aptitude, Pure Maths, Reasoning, English, Computer Knowledge and General Awareness. The exam will be conducted per four tiers. In tier I and II, you will give a computer-based exam whereas, in tier III, a pen-and-paper mode is offered. Finally, in tier IV, a computer proficiency test, skill test, and document verification will be conducted.

⟳

1. Basic Concepts

As a part of classroom training, all the concepts are discussed by our expert faculty thoroughly.

2. Practice Books

📋

3. Home Assignments

Home Assignments are designed to make students revise the concepts, and to encourage them to apply the shortcuts & logic they have learnt in the classroom.

📊

4. Daily Tests
